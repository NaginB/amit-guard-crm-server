## Project Overview: CRM for Security Guard Management

We are developing a **custom CRM system** tailored for a security services company to efficiently manage guards, their assignments, and related operations.

### Core Features

1. **Guard Management**

   - Maintain a complete list of all guards.
   - Add, update, view, and delete guard profiles.
   - Store and manage detailed guard information including:

     - Personal details
     - Bank details
     - Salary & payment records
     - KYC documents
     - Family details and emergency contacts

2. **Attendance & Payroll**

   - Track and record daily attendance of guards.
   - Monitor salary status and payment history for each month.
   - Generate automated salary slips.

3. **Guard Profile Dashboard**

   - A dedicated profile page for each guard showing consolidated details:

     - Personal & professional data
     - Attendance record
     - Salary/payment history
     - Assigned site/location and shift details
     - Inventory assigned

4. **ID Card Management**

   - Automatic generation of guard ID cards with company branding and QR code/barcode support.

5. **Inventory Management**

   - Maintain a centralized inventory system.
   - Track items assigned to each guard (e.g., uniforms, equipment, devices).

6. **Site & Shift Allocation**

   - Assign guards to specific sites/locations.
   - Define shifts (day, night, custom timings).
   - Track guard deployment history.

7. **Billing & Invoicing**

   - Automated monthly bill generation for client sites.
   - Maintain records of payments and dues.
   - Export/download invoices in PDF format.
